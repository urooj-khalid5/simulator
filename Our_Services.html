Our Services

DatA GENERATION

We generate random data based on the parameters provided by the user. This allows you to simulate various scenarios and analyze the outcomes based on your specific requirements.

Metrics calculations

Once the data is generated, our platform performs calculations such as averages, wait time in queue, wait time in the system, and traffic intensity. These calculations provide valuable insights to evaluate system performance.

CHART GENERATION

We provide interactive charts and visualizations based on data and metrics calculations. These visual representations allow you to explore patterns and trends in your data. Examples include Inter-arrival charts, service time charts, and more.
